## 🧭 Quick Analysis

Melusine plays as a **flexible damage dealer** who can switch between single-target Arts NP and AoE Buster NP depending on the fight, making her one of the strongest Buster-looping Lancers when paired with double Koyanskaya + Oberon. Her third skill both transforms her and fully charges her NP gauge, letting her run a Black Grail-style Buster loop with ease. Her main weaknesses are middling natural NP gain from face cards and the fact that her single-target and AoE forms want different support setups.

**Recommended CEs:** The Black Grail (loop), Kaleidoscope (single-target triple NP), NP Gain CEs like Distant Pilgrimage / Mark on a Smiling Face / Magical Girl of Sapphire / Prisma Cosmos (single-target sustain).

**Skill priority:** Ray Horizon (3) → Dragon Heart (1) → Perry Dancer (2)

## 🧭 Quick Analysis

Kama (Avenger) — "Summer Kama" — is a **top-tier AoE Arts looper**, built around a very strong NP refund and Skill 3's NP gauge charge, letting her farm consistently with a wide range of Craft Essences. Her damage can spike hard against static enemies thanks to her NP's DEF-down effect stacking with her unique Flame of Love bonus, and her Skill 1 Evade plus Skill 2 Charm make her surprisingly tanky and useful for boss control in Challenge Quests.

Her main drawbacks: her bonus Charm damage is best saved for the last wave when farming (making earlier waves less efficient), her ramping Flame-of-Love/DEF-down bonuses matter far more against long fights than quick clears, and she competes in an already crowded field of AoE Arts farmers (e.g., Space Ishtar).

**Recommended CEs:** Free event CEs like Ocean Flier / Dive to Blue / Painting Summer, or looping staples like The Black Grail / Heaven's Feel for higher ceiling.

**Skill priority:** Midsummer Sea Mara (3) → the other two roughly even, 10/10/10 recommended if resources allow.
